# Unified Timeline Design

We moved from a multi-section layout (X/Twitter, Blogs, Podcasts, Media as separate blocks) to a single-column unified timeline where all content types are mixed together in Twitter-style cards. Each card shows a one-line summary, source badge, and expands to show full content on click.

**Why:** The original multi-section design was too complex — users had to scan four different sections to grasp the day's updates. A unified timeline reduces cognitive load and feels more like a natural social feed.

**Trade-off:** We lose the ability to quickly scan "only blog posts" or "only tweets." We may add filtering in the future if needed.
